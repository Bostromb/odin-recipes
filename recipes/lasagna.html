Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the best food group. Meat, cheese, and pasta; all in one.

    Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Sausage
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta or cottage cheese
  • Mozzerela cheese
  • Pasta Sauce of your choice
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder

    Steps

  1. Cook noodles
  2. Mix ground beef and sausage and cook thoroughly
  3. Add salt, pepper, and garlic powder to cooking meat
  4. Layer meat, then ricotta cheese, then cooked noodles
  5. Repeat step 4 until no ingredients remain
  6. Cook at 375 degrees for 15 minutes
